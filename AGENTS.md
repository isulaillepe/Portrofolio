# Antigravity Agent Configuration: Project Portfolio

**Target Aesthetic:** "Techno-Minimalist Luxury"
**Primary Persona:** Rajinda – Full-Stack Engineer & Product Marketing Leader.

## 1. Design System & Typography

- **Color Palette:** \* Background: Deep Slate (`#0B0C10`)
  - Surface Cards: Dark Charcoal (`#1F2833`) with 10% opacity (Glassmorphism)
  - Primary Accent: Cyber Sapphire (`#45A29E`)
  - Secondary Accent: Muted Silver (`#C5C6C7`)
- **Typography:** \* Headings: `Playfair Display` (Refined, luxurious, elegant)
  - Body/Tech Stacks: `Inter` or `Montserrat` (Clean, wide-spaced, highly readable)
- **Visual Rules:** \* Maximize negative space. Do not clutter sections.
  - Use subtle fade-in and slide-up animations for scroll reveals.
  - Borders should be 1px solid white at 5% opacity to create a premium edge.

## 2. Content Strategy

- **Tone:** Confident, proactive, and business-acumen oriented.
- **Focus Areas:** Balance technical prowess (Flutter, Node.js, Java) with leadership (AIESEC VP, Team Lead).
- **Rule:** Never use standard, boring bullet points. Convert lists into interactive skill chips, bento-box grids, or horizontal scroll sections.
